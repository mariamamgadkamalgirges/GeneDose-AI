# Roadmap

## Phase 1 – Prototype ✅
- Design the complete UI/UX prototype.
- Define the AML clinical workflow.
- Build an interactive dashboard.

## Phase 2 – Bioinformatics Pipeline
- Import NGS sequencing data.
- Perform quality control.
- Detect genomic variants.
- Annotate AML-related mutations.

## Phase 3 – AI Decision Support
- Interpret genomic mutations.
- Match mutations with clinical guidelines.
- Generate personalized treatment suggestions.

## Phase 4 – Clinical Integration
- Connect with hospital EMR systems.
- Enable secure patient management.
- Generate downloadable clinical reports.

## Phase 5 – Future Development
- Expand to other cancer types.
- Improve AI models using larger datasets.
- Deploy the platform as a cloud-based clinical application.
