# Vision

## GeneDose AI

Our vision is to make precision medicine more accessible for Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML) by combining bioinformatics, Next Generation Sequencing (NGS), and Artificial Intelligence into one clinical decision support platform.

We aim to help oncologists interpret genomic mutations faster, organize patient information efficiently, and generate clear clinical reports while keeping the physician as the final decision maker.

GeneDose AI is designed as a scalable platform that can later expand to support additional cancer types and integrate with hospital electronic medical record (EMR) systems.
