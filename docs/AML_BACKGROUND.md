# Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML)

Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML) is an aggressive hematologic malignancy characterized by the uncontrolled proliferation of immature myeloid cells.

Treatment selection depends on multiple genomic mutations identified using Next Generation Sequencing (NGS).

GeneDose AI is designed to organize genomic findings and support clinicians by presenting clinically relevant information in a structured format.
